Selenium SuperStars Program

A platform for those advocating Selenium to collaborate, automate, and innovate

Apply Now

Come together to advocate for Selenium

The Selenium SuperStars is an ambassador program for programmers, QA engineers, designers, writers, project managers, and anyone who is a Selenium advocate - to celebrate, empower, and support them in their journeys.

As part of the program, Selenium Ambassadors get an opportunity to collaborate and network with peers, exchange insights, showcase expertise, and innovate the world of testing.

Selenium SuperStars: Innovators, Advocates, & More!

Selenium Ambassadors are the driving force behind innovation and excellence in the world of automated testing. And, here is what we look for in them:

Collaboration

Foster a culture of teamwork and bring together diverse perspectives to strengthen the Selenium community

Diversity, Equity & Inclusion

Champions of community that welcomes all, values diversity, and ensures equal opportunities for everyone

Innovation

Continuously push the boundaries of what is possible and drive advancements in automated testing

Advocacy

Deep passion for evangelization, strive to develop transformative solutions that inspire and facilitate positive change

What are the benefits of being a Selenium SuperStar?

As ambassadors of the Selenium project, you will receive unparalleled support to ensure you become a leading thought leader and professional.

Gain Mentorship

Receive mentorship from Selenium Project Leaders, fostering your skills and knowledge for professional and personal growth.

Speaking & Hosting Opportunities

Opportunity to showcase your expertise at Selenium conferences, meetups, and webinars and host sessions to share your insights.

Exclusive Event Benefits & Swags

Get complimentary passes to Selenium conferences and exclusive discounts for your community/team members. Also, grab exclusive Selenium Ambassador swag, including stickers, t-shirts, and hoodies!

Build your Network

Learn from Selenium contributors who share your passion, build connections, and broaden your network for growth & professional opportunities.

Content Feature

Get your content featured on Selenium's social channels, Slack, website, and more.

Event Support & Resources

Collaborate with Selenium contributors and Project Leadership Members as co-speakers, receive support and resources for your community-led initiatives.




What will you do as a Selenium SuperStar?

Evangelize Selenium through events & webinars

Host a meet-up, deliver a talk, volunteer for Selenium Ambassador, and organize or participate in in-person and virtual events/webinars globally evangelize Selenium

Contribute to Selenium's blog, documentations, and more

Share your expertise, through contributions to regular blog posts, social media posts, and documentation to enrich the Selenium ecosystem.

Support the community

Engage with the Selenium community through community channel, resolve queries, support the Selenium Developer Group, and participate in discussions.

Become a Selenium thought leader

Elevate your profile, contribute code, and actively shape the Selenium project's future.

Eligibility Criteria

Selenium SuperStar applicants must be:

  • 18 years old or older
  • Committed to Selenium’s values
  • Active leaders in the community with at least 1 year of experience organizing events, speaking at events, creating resources like blogs or videos and/or mentoring others
  • Willing to actively participate and commit towards the program
